Two kitchens, one fire

What's actually in the pot

Mandi · Yemeni-style

Smoked, not stewed.

Meat goes into a sealed pit over hot coals, hung above the rice so every drop of fat falls straight into the grain below. No sauce poured on after — the smoke is already in there.

Biryani · Dum-cooked

Sealed and slow-cooked.

Marinated meat and parboiled rice, layered by hand, sealed under dough, and left on a low flame until the steam does the rest. We open the pot only when yours is ready.
